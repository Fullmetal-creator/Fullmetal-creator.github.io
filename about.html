About What's Your Engine

What's Your Engine is a fun running performance calculator that compares your running ability with different types of cars.

By entering your running distance, time, age, and body type, the system calculates your performance level and matches it with a vehicle engine type.

The goal is simple — make running more exciting and relatable by connecting athletic performance with automotive power.

Whether you're a casual runner or a competitive racer, discover what engine you truly are.
